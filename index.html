🌴 Our Story

Kerala Foodie Centre was born from a simple longing — the taste of home. Living in Pune, we missed authentic Kerala flavours like soft appams, rich coconut curries, and spicy naadan dishes.

What started as cooking for friends and family slowly became a home kitchen built on one promise — freshly prepared food made only after receiving your order.

Every dish is made with fresh ingredients, traditional spices, and the warmth of a Kerala home.
